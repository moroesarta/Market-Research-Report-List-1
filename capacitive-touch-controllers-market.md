<p><p>The capacitive touch controllers market is divided into two main types: single-touch (self-capacitive) controllers and multi-touch (mutual capacitive) controllers. Single-touch controllers detect input from one finger at a time, primarily used in basic interfaces like smartphones and simple devices. In contrast, multi-touch controllers can sense multiple simultaneous touches, enabling complex gestures and interactive experiences, commonly found in advanced smartphones, tablets, and touch-enabled devices, enhancing user interaction through improved responsiveness and functionality.</p></p>
