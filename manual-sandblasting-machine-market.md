<p><p>The manual sandblasting machine market is divided into two main types: dry and wet sandblasting. Dry sandblasting uses compressed air to propel abrasive materials, making it ideal for removing paint and rust from surfaces. It is known for its efficiency and effectiveness on various substrates. In contrast, wet sandblasting incorporates water to suppress dust and reduce airborne contaminants, providing a cleaner process. This method is often favored for its ability to minimize environmental impact while delivering a smoother finish.</p></p>
