<p><p>The Mechanical Impurity Tester market includes techniques such as the Weight Method and Membrane Filtration. The Weight Method involves measuring the mass of impurities in a sample, providing a direct assessment of contamination levels. In contrast, Membrane Filtration uses a filter to capture impurities from a liquid sample, allowing for quantitative analysis based on the retained particles. Both methods are essential for quality control in various industries, ensuring compliance with standards for product purity and safety.</p></p>
