<p><p>The powered surgical tools market includes three primary types: electric-powered, battery-powered, and pneumatic-powered tools. </p><p>Electric-powered tools utilize electricity for consistent and strong performance, ideal for complex surgeries. Battery-powered tools offer portability and freedom of movement, making them suitable for procedures in various settings. Pneumatic-powered tools rely on compressed air, delivering high power and speed, often used in environments where electricity may not be available. Each type serves distinct surgical needs, enhancing efficiency and precision in various procedures.</p></p>
