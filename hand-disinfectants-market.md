<p><p>The hand disinfectants market is primarily categorized into two types: alcohol-based and non-alcohol-based disinfectants. Alcohol-based disinfectants typically contain ethanol or isopropyl alcohol, effectively killing germs and are widely favored for their rapid action. In contrast, non-alcohol-based disinfectants often utilize quaternary ammonium compounds or plant extracts, appealing to those seeking alcohol-free options, particularly for sensitive skin. Both types play essential roles in maintaining hygiene in various settings, including healthcare, education, and public spaces.</p></p>
