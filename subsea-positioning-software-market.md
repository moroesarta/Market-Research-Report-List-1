<p><strong>The Subsea Positioning Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Long Baseline System</li><li>Short Baseline System</li><li>Ultra Short Baseline System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Subsea positioning software is crucial for underwater operations, and it primarily falls into three market types: Long Baseline System (LBL), Short Baseline System (SBL), and Ultra Short Baseline System (USBL). LBL uses multiple transponders on the seabed to provide precise positioning over long distances. SBL relies on fewer transponders and is suited for shorter ranges, offering faster setup. USBL utilizes a single transducer for real-time positioning, ideal for dynamic operations in close proximity, making it versatile for various underwater tasks.</p></p>
