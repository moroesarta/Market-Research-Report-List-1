<p><strong>The Portable Gas Chromatograph Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas-liquid Chromatograph</li><li>Gas-solid Chromatograph</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable gas chromatograph market is primarily divided into two types: gas-liquid chromatograph (GLC) and gas-solid chromatograph (GSC). GLC separates volatile compounds based on their interactions with a liquid stationary phase, making it ideal for analyzing gases and vapors. In contrast, GSC utilizes a solid stationary phase to selectively adsorb gases, allowing for the analysis of heavier hydrocarbons and trace components. Both types cater to applications in environmental monitoring, food safety, and industrial processes, enhancing on-site analysis capabilities.</p></p>
