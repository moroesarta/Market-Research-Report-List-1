<p><strong>The Industrial Safety Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spring-loaded Safety Valves</li><li>Pilot-operated Safety Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial safety valves market includes two primary types: spring-loaded safety valves and pilot-operated safety valves. Spring-loaded safety valves use a spring mechanism to automatically release excess pressure, ensuring operational safety in various industrial processes. In contrast, pilot-operated safety valves utilize a pressure sensor to activate a larger valve, providing greater control and efficiency in high-pressure systems. Both types play crucial roles in preventing equipment failures and ensuring compliance with safety regulations across industries.</p></p>
