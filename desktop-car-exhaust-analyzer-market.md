<p><ul><li>Capacitive</li><li>Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Desktop Car Exhaust Analyzer market features two primary types: capacitive and semiconductor analyzers. Capacitive analyzers utilize electrical capacitance to measure gas concentrations and provide rapid analysis with high sensitivity. They are effective for various exhaust gases. In contrast, semiconductor analyzers rely on changes in conductivity within semiconductor materials to detect specific gases, offering compact designs and lower costs. Both types serve critical roles in emissions testing and regulatory compliance, catering to automotive manufacturers and environmental monitoring agencies.</p></p>
