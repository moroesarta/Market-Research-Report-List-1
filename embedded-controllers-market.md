<p><strong>The Embedded Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simple Programmable Logic Devices</li><li>Complex Programmable Logic Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The embedded controllers market is divided into two main types: Simple Programmable Logic Devices (SPLDs) and Complex Programmable Logic Devices (CPLDs). SPLDs are used for basic logic tasks, offering limited programmability and simpler designs, making them cost-effective and suitable for small-scale applications. In contrast, CPLDs possess greater complexity, supporting more extensive programmable logic functions. This advanced capability allows for intricate digital designs, making CPLDs ideal for applications requiring higher performance and flexibility, such as telecommunications and automotive systems.</p></p>
