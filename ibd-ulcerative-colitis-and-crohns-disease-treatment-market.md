<p><p>The IBD treatment market encompasses various therapeutic classes for managing Ulcerative Colitis and Crohn's Disease. TNF inhibitors, such as infliximab and adalimumab, target inflammation by blocking tumor necrosis factor. Aminosalicylates, like mesalamine, reduce inflammation directly in the intestinal lining. Integrin antagonists, such as vedolizumab, prevent immune cells from inflaming the gut. Corticosteroids, like prednisone, provide rapid relief by suppressing the immune response, although long-term use is limited due to side effects. Each class offers distinct benefits for patient management.</p></p>
