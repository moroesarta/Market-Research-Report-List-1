<p><p>The GPS trailer tracker market is divided into two main types: wired and wireless GPS trailer trackers. Wired GPS trailer trackers are permanently installed, connected to the trailer's power source, ensuring continuous tracking without the need for battery replacements. In contrast, wireless GPS trailer trackers are portable, using battery power or solar energy, allowing for easy installation and mobility. Both types offer real-time tracking and monitoring capabilities, enhancing fleet management and security for trailer operators.</p></p>
