<p><p>The BAW SMR (Bulk Acoustic Wave Surface Micromachined Resonator) filter market consists of four main types: </p><p>1. **Ladder Type Filter**: Utilizes a series of resonators connected in a ladder-like formation, offering good selectivity.</p><p>2. **Lattice Type Filter**: Features a network configuration providing enhanced frequency response and rejection capabilities.</p><p>3. **Stacked Crystal Filter**: Combines multiple resonators in layers for improved performance in compact designs.</p><p>4. **Coupled Resonator Filter**: Engages multiple resonators that interact to achieve desired frequency characteristics, ideal for high-performance applications.</p></p>
