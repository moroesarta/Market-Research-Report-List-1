<p><strong>The Automatic Solid Waste Collection Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity System</li><li>Full Vacuum System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic Solid Waste Collection Systems (ASWCS) use two primary market types: Gravity Systems and Full Vacuum Systems. </p><p>Gravity systems rely on sloped pipelines to transport waste by gravitational force, making them energy-efficient and low-maintenance. They are suitable for urban areas with proper elevation. </p><p>In contrast, Full Vacuum Systems use suction to move waste through sealed pipes, allowing for easier waste collection from various locations without relying on gravity. This system is ideal for areas with complex topography or dense urban settings.</p></p>
