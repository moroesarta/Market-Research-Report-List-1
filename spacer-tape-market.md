<p><strong>The Spacer Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-sided Adhesive</li><li>Double-sided Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spacer Tape market is divided into two primary types: single-sided adhesive tape and double-sided tape. Single-sided adhesive tape features adhesive on one side, making it ideal for mounting, protecting, and insulating applications where only one surface requires adhesion. In contrast, double-sided tape has adhesive on both sides, allowing for bonding two surfaces together without visible support. Both types are essential in various industries, including construction, automotive, and electronics, due to their versatility and strong adhesive properties.</p></p>
