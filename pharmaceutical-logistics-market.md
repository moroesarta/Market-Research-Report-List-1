<p><ul><li>Cold Chain Logistics</li><li>Non-cold Chain Logistics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pharmaceutical logistics encompasses two primary market types: cold chain logistics and non-cold chain logistics. Cold chain logistics involves the transportation and storage of temperature-sensitive pharmaceuticals, such as vaccines and biologics, requiring strict temperature control throughout the supply chain to maintain efficacy. Non-cold chain logistics, on the other hand, deals with drugs that are stable at room temperature, focusing on standard shipping and storage methods. Both types are crucial for ensuring the integrity and availability of pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
