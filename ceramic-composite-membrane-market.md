<p><p>The ceramic composite membrane market is categorized into two main types: flat-sheet membranes and pipe membranes. Flat-sheet membranes are widely used in various filtration processes due to their large surface area and ease of integration into existing systems. They are ideal for applications like wastewater treatment and water purification. On the other hand, pipe membranes are designed for cylindrical configurations, making them suitable for specific industrial applications, such as gas separation and chemical processing, where space efficiency is crucial.</p></p>
