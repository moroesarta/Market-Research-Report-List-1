<p><p>The silicone adhesive sheet market is segmented into single-sided and double-sided types. Single-sided adhesive sheets have adhesive on one side, making them ideal for applications where only one surface needs to be bonded, while the reverse remains exposed. In contrast, double-sided adhesive sheets feature adhesive on both sides, allowing for bonding between two surfaces, providing enhanced versatility. These products are used in various industries, including automotive, electronics, and construction, due to their excellent heat resistance and durability.</p></p>
