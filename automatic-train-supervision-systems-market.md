<p><ul><li>Central System</li><li>Subsystem</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic Train Supervision Systems (ATSS) encompass two main market types: Central Systems and Subsystems. Central Systems serve as the core management hubs, overseeing train operations, scheduling, and real-time monitoring across entire networks, ensuring efficient service delivery. Subsystems, on the other hand, include localized components such as train control systems, signaling technologies, and communication interfaces that support the central infrastructure by providing critical functionality at specific locations. Together, they enhance reliability and operational efficiency in rail transport.</p></p>
