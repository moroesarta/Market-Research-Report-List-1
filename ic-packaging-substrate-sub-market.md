<p><strong>The IC Packaging Substrate SUB Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Organic Substrate</li><li>Inorganic Substrate</li><li>Composite Substrate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IC Packaging Substrate market comprises three main types: organic substrates, inorganic substrates, and composite substrates. Organic substrates, typically made from materials like polyimide, enable high-density interconnects and are widely used for their flexibility and cost-effectiveness. Inorganic substrates, often composed of materials such as ceramics or glass, provide superior thermal and electrical performance, making them ideal for high-power applications. Composite substrates combine properties of both organic and inorganic materials, offering enhanced performance and versatility, catering to diverse electronic packaging needs.</p></p>
