<p><p>The blood filter market is categorized into three main types based on transfusion needs: whole blood transfusion, platelet transfusion, and red cell transfusion. Whole blood transfusion involves the transfer of all blood components, typically used in major surgeries or trauma cases. Platelet transfusion focuses on improving clotting capabilities in patients with low platelet counts, often seen in cancer treatments. Red cell transfusion, the most common type, addresses anemia and blood loss by restoring hemoglobin levels and oxygen-carrying capacity in patients.</p></p>
