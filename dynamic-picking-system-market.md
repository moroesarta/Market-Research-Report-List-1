<p><p>Dynamic Picking Systems are categorized into two main market types: Fully Automatic Systems and Semi-Automatic Systems. Fully Automatic Systems utilize advanced robotics and automation technology to handle the entire picking process, enhancing efficiency and reducing labor costs. In contrast, Semi-Automatic Systems combine automated processes with human intervention, allowing operators to oversee the picking while benefiting from automation. This hybrid approach provides flexibility and can be tailored to various operational needs, making it suitable for diverse warehouse environments.</p></p>
