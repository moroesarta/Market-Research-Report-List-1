<p><p>Circular weft knitting machines are categorized into two main types: single circular and double circular weft knitting machines. Single circular weft knitting machines utilize a single set of needles, producing a tubular fabric efficiently suited for lighter materials. In contrast, double circular weft knitting machines employ two sets of needles, allowing for thicker and more complex fabrics, enhancing versatility in production. These machines cater to various textile demands, influencing the market's technological advancements and diversification.</p></p>
