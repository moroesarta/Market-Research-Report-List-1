<p><ul><li>Active Terahertz</li><li>Passive Terahertz</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The terahertz (THz) detection and inspection market is categorized into two main types: active terahertz and passive terahertz. Active terahertz systems emit signals and analyze the reflected data, enabling detailed imaging and measurements, often used in security scanning and material characterization. In contrast, passive terahertz systems detect naturally occurring terahertz radiation from objects, making them ideal for thermal imaging and environmental monitoring. Both types are essential for applications in security, quality control, and non-destructive testing.</p></p>
