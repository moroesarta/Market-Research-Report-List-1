<p><ul><li>Mono</li><li>Dual-Channel</li><li>Stereo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile phone loudspeaker market consists of three types: mono, dual-channel, and stereo. Mono speakers produce sound from a single channel, offering basic audio output suitable for calls and simple alert tones. Dual-channel speakers enhance sound quality by providing two distinct audio channels, delivering a richer experience, especially for media playback. Stereo speakers further elevate audio fidelity by creating spatial sound through two separate channels, providing immersive experiences ideal for music, videos, and gaming. Each type caters to varying user preferences and usage scenarios.</p></p>
