<p><ul><li>DC Charging`</li><li>AC Charging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EV charging equipment market is primarily divided into two types: DC Charging and AC Charging. DC Charging, also known as fast charging, provides direct current directly to the vehicle's battery, enabling quicker charging times, typically suitable for commercial applications and public charging stations. AC Charging delivers alternating current and is generally slower, commonly used in residential settings and workplace charging, making it ideal for overnight charging. Both types play crucial roles in expanding electric vehicle infrastructure and user convenience.</p></p>
