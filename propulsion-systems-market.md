<p><strong>The Propulsion Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Breathing Engines</li><li>Non-Air Breathing Engines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The propulsion systems market is divided into air-breathing and non-air-breathing engines. Air-breathing engines, such as turbojets and turbofans, use atmospheric oxygen for combustion, making them efficient for subsonic and supersonic flight. Non-air-breathing engines, like rocket engines, carry both fuel and oxidizer, enabling operation in space or environments without atmosphere. Each type serves distinct applications, with air-breathing engines typically used in commercial and military aircraft, while non-air-breathing engines are essential for space exploration and satellite deployment.</p></p>
