<p><p>The Online Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) equipment market is primarily segmented into two main types: 2D AOI and 3D AOI equipment. </p><p>2D AOI equipment captures images of components on a PCB and compares them against predefined specifications, effectively detecting surface defects and misalignments. </p><p>In contrast, 3D AOI equipment utilizes advanced imaging techniques to provide depth perception, allowing for more comprehensive inspections of solder joints and component placements. The distinction lies in their imaging capabilities, impacting inspection thoroughness and effectiveness.</p></p>
